About

AI is not only
for tech people.

In 2026, AI is profoundly transforming professions and organizations. Many still believe it belongs only to technical profiles. My career tells precisely the opposite story.

It all begins in Luxembourg. A European childhood, open to the world. Then Buenos Aires, in 1994. Five years of full immersion in Argentina. Back in Europe, Paris became my base. With a chapter in Moscow, from 2011 to 2012. Thirty years shaped by adaptation, boldness and curiosity.

Twenty years in the most prestigious American groups. Eighteen years at McKinsey & Company, in the Communications Department. Then J.P. Morgan. Then Warner Bros. Discovery. Four global CEOs, three of them foreign, in under three years. Those years forged a unique understanding of the executive world. Anticipation. Rigor. Communication under pressure.

Then Artificial Intelligence entered my life. As an obvious fit. As a genuine passion.

I had the privilege of learning from the very best:

  • ◈ The best Communicators — McKinsey & Company
  • ◈ The best Leaders — Global CEOs
  • ◈ The best AI Consultants — Studeria.

The RS6776 certification confirmed a strong conviction. The most powerful tools must become accessible to everyone.

I don't believe in unrealistic promises.
I believe in useful, concrete AI, intelligently integrated into daily work.

Because professionals who embrace AI will have a competitive edge.
Even without knowing how to code.
